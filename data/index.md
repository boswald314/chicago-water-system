# Data: CSO pumping-station activity log

MWRD's own event log of combined-sewer-overflow discharges at its six CSO pumping stations —
one row per confirmed discharge event with the volume released. Source: MWRD *Pumping Station
Activity* workbook (data updated **July 6, 2026**; obtained July 2026). MWRD's caveat, verbatim:
*"Although the map pages may indicate that additional CSOs have occurred within the last week,
the data on this sheet is not updated until the volumes have been confirmed."*

**Download: [mwrd-ps-cso-activity.csv](mwrd-ps-cso-activity.csv)** (1,039 rows: 988 discharge
events, 50 explicit "No Discharge" year markers, 1 flagged anomaly). The original `.xls` is kept
in the local archive's `sources/` folder.

## Conversion notes & caveats

- **Faithful conversion:** raw `year`, `date`, and `volume` cells are preserved alongside
  normalized columns (`date_iso`, `span_days`, `volume_mg`); every non-trivial parse is
  recorded in `flags` (multi-day ranges like "4/30-5/1/19" keep their span; one "11/1713"
  missing-slash typo is flagged; three Excel-serial cells are flagged).
- **Units:** Westchester's sheet reports gallons; `volume_mg` normalizes to million gallons.
- **One unexplained source oddity kept as-is:** Racine Avenue's sheet contains an event dated
  June 29, **1931** — eight years before the station opened (its 39th Street predecessor era);
  it is preserved verbatim and flagged, not corrected.
- **One anomaly:** a dateless 32,000 value on the 122nd St sheet (excel row 119), preserved
  with `row_type=anomaly` and excluded from the totals above.
- North Branch context — what these events are and why the station discharges proactively —
  is in [doc 08](../docs/08-north-branch-pumping-station.html); systemwide CSO context is in
  [doc 14](../docs/14-cso-outfalls-discharges.html).
